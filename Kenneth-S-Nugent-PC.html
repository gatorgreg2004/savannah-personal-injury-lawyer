In the heart of Georgia's oldest city, where Spanish moss drapes over oak-lined streets and historic squares offer moments of tranquility, accidents can still disrupt the lives of Savannah's residents and visitors. When misfortune strikes, whether on the busy lanes of Abercorn Street or in the quiet corners of Forsyth Park, Kenneth S. A Legacy of Legal Excellence in Savannah


For over 40 years, Kenneth S. Nugent, P.C. has been a beacon of hope for injury victims across Savannah and the surrounding Coastal Empire. Our firm's deep roots in the community, combined with our extensive legal expertise, make us the go-to choice for those seeking justice after an injury.


Our Comprehensive Legal Services

At Kenneth S. Nugent, P.C., we understand that each case is as unique as the historic squares that dot our beautiful city. Our experienced attorneys handle a wide range of personal injury cases, including:


  1. Car Accidents
  2. Truck Accidents
  3. Slip and Fall Injuries
  4. Medical Malpractice
  5. Wrongful Death
  6. Workers' Compensation Claims


Let's delve into how we can assist you in each of these areas.


Car Accidents: Navigating Savannah's Busy Streets


Savannah's charm lies in its historic layout, but its streets can be treacherous for the unprepared. From fender benders on Broughton Street to major collisions on the Truman Parkway, our team has seen it all. Common accident hotspots include:


  • The intersection of Abercorn Street and DeRenne Avenue
  • Victory Drive during rush hour
  • Bay Street in the bustling historic district
  • Skidaway Road near Bacon Park
  • The Talmadge Memorial Bridge approach


If you've been injured in a car accident anywhere in Savannah, from the suburbs of Georgetown to the shores of Tybee Island, our experienced attorneys are here to help. We'll investigate the accident scene, gather crucial evidence, and build a strong case to ensure you receive fair compensation for your injuries, lost wages, and pain and suffering. So please get in touch with a personal injury attorney as soon as possible!


Truck Accidents: Taking on Big Rigs on I-95 and Beyond


With the Port of Savannah being one of the busiest in the nation, our city sees a constant flow of commercial trucks. While these vehicles are vital to our economy, they can pose significant dangers on our roads. Truck accidents often occur on:


  • I-95, especially near the I-16 interchange
  • Highway 80 leading to Tybee Island
  • Dean Forest Road near the port
  • Jimmy DeLoach Parkway
  • East President Street near the port facilities


Truck accident cases can be complex, often involving multiple parties including the driver, trucking company, and insurance providers. Our experienced attorneys know how to navigate these intricacies, ensuring that all responsible parties are held accountable for your injuries.


Slip and Fall: Protecting Your Rights in Savannah's Historic District and Beyond


Savannah's beautiful historic district, with its cobblestone streets and antique buildings, can sometimes pose hidden dangers. Slip and fall accidents can occur anywhere, from the shops on River Street to the halls of Oglethorpe Mall. Common locations for these incidents include:


  • The uneven sidewalks along Bull Street
  • Wet floors in restaurants on Broughton Street
  • Poorly maintained staircases in historic buildings on Jones Street
  • Dimly lit parking lots near Savannah Mall
  • Slippery conditions at public pools like the Daffin Park Pool


Our attorneys understand the nuances of premises liability law in Georgia. We'll work tirelessly to prove negligence on the part of property owners and secure the compensation you need for your recovery.


Medical Malpractice: Holding Healthcare Providers Accountable


Savannah is home to several excellent healthcare facilities, including Memorial Health University Medical Center and St. Joseph's Hospital. However, even the best hospitals can sometimes fall short of the standard of care. Our medical malpractice team handles cases involving:


  • Surgical errors
  • Misdiagnosis or delayed diagnosis
  • Medication errors
  • Birth injuries
  • Nursing home neglect


Medical malpractice cases require a deep understanding of both law and medicine. Our attorneys work closely with medical experts to build strong cases that clearly demonstrate how healthcare providers failed in their duty of care.


Wrongful Death: Seeking Justice for Grieving Families


Losing a loved one is always devastating, but when that loss is due to someone else's negligence, the pain is compounded by a sense of injustice. Whether the death occurred due to a tragic accident on I-16 or medical negligence at a local hospital, our compassionate attorneys are here to help families seek justice and financial security.

We handle wrongful death cases arising from various circumstances, including:


  • Fatal car or truck accidents
  • Workplace accidents
  • Medical malpractice
  • Defective products
  • Criminal acts


While no amount of money can replace your loved one, a wrongful death claim can provide financial stability and a sense of closure. Our team will handle all aspects of your case with sensitivity and determination, allowing you to focus on healing.


Workers' Compensation: Protecting Savannah's Workforce


From the longshoremen at the Port of Savannah to the hospitality workers in our world-famous hotels, Savannah's diverse workforce is the backbone of our economy. When workplace injuries occur, whether in the industrial complexes along Highway 21 or in the kitchens of River Street restaurants, workers have the right to compensation.

Our workers' compensation attorneys handle cases involving:


  • Construction accidents
  • Repetitive stress injuries
  • Occupational illnesses
  • Industrial accidents
  • Office injuries


Navigating the workers' compensation system can be challenging, especially when employers or insurance companies dispute claims. Our experienced team will guide you through the process, ensuring you receive the benefits you're entitled to under Georgia law.

  5. Personalized Attention: Unlike some large firms where clients become just another case number, we provide individualized attention to each client. Your case will be handled by experienced attorneys who are always accessible to answer your questions and address your concerns.
  6. Resources to Take on Big Insurance Companies: Insurance companies often try to minimize payouts or deny claims altogether. With our resources and experience, we level the playing field, ensuring you're not taken advantage of during this vulnerable time.
