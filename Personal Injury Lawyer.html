Savannah's unique blend of old town roads, busy thoroughfares, and tourist traffic creates a complex environment for drivers. Common accident trouble areas include:


  • The intersection of Abercorn Street and DeRenne Avenue
  • I-16 and its exits
  • Bay Street in the historic district
  • Victory Drive during peak traffic times


Kenneth S. Nugent, P.C.'s team of knowledgeable attorneys recognizes these local nuances, which can be vital in building a strong case for accident victims.
